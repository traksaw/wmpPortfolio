Waskar Miguel Paulino

Software Engineer | Web Development Expert | Creative Architect

Beginnings

Waskar Miguel Paulino, a first-generation Dominican-American, began his journey with a passion for education and music. He earned a Bachelor of Education in Spanish and a minor in music from Kutztown University. Teaching underserved communities and producing live shows fueled his commitment to serving others.

Discovery

While at Apple as a Certified Technician, Waskar discovered the power of technology and liberal arts. This sparked his self-taught coding journey, starting with Swift and expanding to HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. His skills deepened through his work with Resilient Coders.

Vision

Waskar’s mission is to create innovative solutions that empower people to achieve their goals. He envisions a supportive community where individuals feel encouraged to pursue their dreams, knowing they have advocates dedicated to their success.
